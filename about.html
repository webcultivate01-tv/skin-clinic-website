Our Journey

12 Years of Transforming Skin

Founded in 2012 by Dr. Meera Joshi, Lumière Skin Clinic was born from a simple belief — everyone deserves healthy, glowing skin. What started as a small dermatology practice has grown into one of the most trusted skin care destinations in the region.

We blend the precision of modern dermatology with a deep respect for each patient's individuality. No two skin journeys are the same, and neither are our treatment plans.
